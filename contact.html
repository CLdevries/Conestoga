FAQs - Nelson Mandela

Question Answer
When was Nelson Mandela's birthday? 18 July.
How old was Nelson Mandela? The late former President Mandela was 95 when he died.
What were the names of Nelson Mandela’s parents? His father was Nkosi (Chief) Mphakanyiswa Mandela and his mother was Nosekeni Mandela.
When did Nelson Mandela’s parents die? His father died in 1930 and his mother died in 1968.
What does the name "Madiba" mean? Madiba is the name of the Thembu clan to which Mr Mandela belonged, used as a sign of respect and affection.
How many brothers and sisters did Nelson Mandela have? Mr Mandela had three full sisters, three half-brothers, and six half-sisters.
How tall was Nelson Mandela? 1.84m tall.
Who are Nelson Mandela’s biological family? Mr Mandela is survived by his daughters Makaziwe, Zenani, and Zindzi, 17 grandchildren, and 13 great-grandchildren.
